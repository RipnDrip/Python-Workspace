• Liability Insurance

    From $500/yr

    Liability coverage pays for property damage and/or injuries to another person caused by an accident. Required by state in order to operate vehicle.

  • Uninsured and Underinsured Motorist Coverage

    From $50/yr

    Uninsured motorist insurance protects you if you're in an accident with an at-fault driver who doesn't carry liability insurance.

  • Comprehensive Coverage

    From $140/yr

    Helps cover damage to your car from things like theft, fire, hail or vandalism. If your car is damaged by a covered peril, comprehensive coverage may help pay to repair or replace your vehicle. Has a deductible.

  • Collision Coverage

    From $400/yr

    Helps pay to repair or replace your vehicle if it's damaged or destroyed in an accident with another car, regardless of who is at fault. Has deductible.

  • Medical Payment Coverage

    From $100/yr

    Helps pay your or your passengers' medical expenses if you're injured in a car accident, regardless of who caused the accident.

  • Personal Injury Protection (PIP)

    From $1000/yr

    Helps pay for your medical expenses after accident. PIP may also help cover other expenses incurred because of your injuries, such as loss of income.
